On dreams

What if we are God’s dream and when He sleeps, the universe awakens, and when He wakes up, the universe ends. What if we are all a small part of God’s mind and He is dreaming all of this right now and we are all living in a dream. What if instead of God being in our hearts what if we are in God’s heart. I wonder if God goes through multiple cycles of REM sleep.
