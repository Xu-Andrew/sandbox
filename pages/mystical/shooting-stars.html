On shooting stars

“Courage!”
“Yes?” came a soft reply from somewhere.
She turned around to see Courage on the rooftop. Courage momentarily tilted his head down from the stars to look at her.
“What are you doing up there?”
“The sky is full of shooting stars tonight,” said Courage. “I don’t have enough wishes to use them all right now so I’m saving the ones I can’t use. I’m writing down their transversal velocities and orthogonal projections to find them again when I need them.”
A bright light shot through the sky and lit up the heavens for a fleeting moment.
